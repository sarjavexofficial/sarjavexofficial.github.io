Products

What we are building

iPhone (iOS) Coming soon to the App Store

Memory Twin

A memory notebook for the AI era — switch AIs, keep your memory.

An iPhone app that turns your journal, notes about people, and AI conversations into long-term memory, and lets AI recall them for you — always with sources. Import your ChatGPT or Claude export files, and your important decisions, promises, and insights stay with you even when you switch AI apps.

Core features are free (in-app purchases available), with a 7-day Pro trial

  • 🔍

    AI search with sources

    Ask “what did we decide back then?” and the AI answers with the records it drew from.

  • 📬

    Today Recall

    Promises coming due, people you haven’t talked to in a while — the app proactively surfaces what you should remember today.

  • 📥

    AI history import

    Merge official ChatGPT and Claude exports into your timeline. Forgotten promises are discovered automatically.

  • 🔒

    Local-first design

    All records stay on your device. When you use AI, only relevant excerpts are sent and never stored on our servers. Backups are encrypted on-device — even we cannot read them.
